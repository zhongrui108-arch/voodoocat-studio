About the Studio

Toys that
get you.

We make limited-run, design-led collectible figures for teens & collectors who want their space to scream personality. Not mass-produced. Not kid stuff. Not boring decor.

Every drop is a character study — dark, playful, a little unhinged, and completely impossible to ignore. From angel-devil bunny duos to rose-ward doctor characters, each piece is a world you want to live in.

We're building the brand that finally fills the gap between premium design objects and tactile, interactive toys — for the adults who want both.
